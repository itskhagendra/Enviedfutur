About US

Envied FutuR is an innovative group whose primary goal is to transform the fashion industry, removing dependence on crude power sources and establishing widespread use of renewable energy. To attain our aims and objectives, we convert waste plastic into textiles to make premium, sustainable, and fashionable clothing. On this basis, we can call out the general public to join us in affordably saving the planet. i.e., a person may not be able to afford an EV, but a hoodie is still within reach. Imagine buying a hoodie, and a tree is planted to complement your purchase - This is a single instance of the power of Envied FutuR. We have been operating within the fashion industry since 1992. Over the past decades, we've partnered with over 50+ companies, sold around 400 tons of sustainable clothing, and created a massive community of heroes living wild to save their planet. Please contact us or read our Pitch deck to learn more about us.
